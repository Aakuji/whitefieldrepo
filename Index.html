ABOUT Established in 2003, WHITEFIELD INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE (WIC), is an outstanding center of knowledge and academic excellence currently running excellently running under the management of Raktakali Education Foundation (REF). Ideally located at Nayabazar, Town Planning, Kathmandu Whitefiled International College is a unique center of learning where each and every student can have the best opportunity for exploring and developing his\her full potentials. The principle of learning by doing underlies every major discipline of Whitefield’s education system.
